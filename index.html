How it works

Brief, build, ship.

Three steps from idea to production. Our agent stack handles the execution — you focus on the vision.

01 / BRIEF

Share your vision

Tell us what to build. Our Architect agent converts your brief into a full technical specification — stack, schema, API endpoints, timeline — in hours, not days. You review, approve, and we start.

02 / BUILD

Agents build it

Forge codes. Guardian tests and deploys. You watch real-time progress in your Folio client portal. No status meetings. No surprise delays. Every milestone documented as it ships.

03 / SHIP

Live in days

Your product goes live, monitored 24/7, and handed over with documentation. Invoice triggered on delivery — not on a billing cycle. What you see on day one is what you pay for.
